AboutAbout

Leopold Lael is the Chief Restaurant Officer and Corporate SVP, International Operated Markets (IOM) at McDonald’s. In this dual role, he is leading the organization responsible for driving growth and accountability in 17 wholly owned markets outside the U.S.. A member of the McDonald’s family for more than 25 years, he has built a reputation for his:

● Investment-led approach and outstanding commercial results
● Unwavering focus on the customer experience, with a proven impact on frequency
● Collaborative and honest leadership style, as well as a passion for creating an exceptional working environment for McDonald’s team members at all levels of the organization

Following progressive leadership roles in the UK finance organization, including SVP and Chief Financial Officer for UK and Northern Europe, Paul was appointed CEO of McDonald’s UK and Ireland in 2015. Under his stewardship, the business experienced significant and consistent growth, driven by a customer-centric focus, meticulous approach to service excellence, and investments in initiatives that truly matter to employees, customers, franchisees, and suppliers.

Notably, Paul brought a renewed focus on creating a truly inclusive company culture in which team members are engaged and motivated to achieve the company’s vision of being the best-loved restaurant company. Through new DEI efforts and dramatic improvements in pay, benefits, and working environments, the business achieved gender balance at every level of the organization, and was nationally recognized as the Top Employer for Working Mums and the Best Employer for Mental Health.
